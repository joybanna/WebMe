Stealth Prototype
> Github source file <
Stealth Prototype is a Stealth Game.Players will have to sneak through obstacles and watch out for enemies that can be seen to reach the objective of the game.
When playing, the player will be a green box in the middle of the screen, the upper left corner will tell you the status of the player, can use the scroll mouse to zoom in and out.
If the player is detected by the enemy, the game will be over.
When the player goes through various obstacles and can find the end of the game, they win.
