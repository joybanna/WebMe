Find Me is a 3D Hide and Seek Game. Multiplayer systems powered by Unet.
Unet lobby and room systems
The players will be divided into 2 factions Hiding and Hunter.
The Hiding can hide in an object.The Hunter can shoot a gun.
When the hiding is hit by the hunter, The hunter is win or time out the hiding is a win.
