Typing Typhoon (NSC)
Typing Typhoon is a 2D Typing Game. Which has been supported by NECTEC, in order to encourage pupils and students to learn and practice their skills in developing software.
This game is suitable for players practicing keyboard typing and just for fun. It has 2 modes story and Arcade
The story mode is easier than the arcade. it can choose a level for play
Arcade mode is harder than Story. it a random level word to typing for play. it collects scores to show the leaderboard.
