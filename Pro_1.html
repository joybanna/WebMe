Monopoly Prototype
Monopoly Prototype is a Monopoly Game. You can choose a character. And in each character having different abilities to achieve the objective of the game.
  • A female character can roll 1-20 point
  • A police character can roll 1-20 point
  • A cowboy character can roll 1-20 point
Can play online multiplayer, create a room, or random join room other players.
Can limit player in the room it yourself (max 4 players) and play if all member ready to play.
The map will have random traps. And the players must go to the last point exactly.
Players will randomly play order. When reaching your turn, a random number will be according to the character you choose. And will alternate like this until the end of the game.
The player who can make it to the last point wins the game.
